What is WebP?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google. It was designed to replace older formats like JPEG, PNG, and GIF by offering better compression and quality. It supports both lossy and lossless compression, making it a solid choice serving images online. WebP also includes support for transparent backgrounds.
